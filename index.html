Services

A well developed and functioning civil registration system ensures the registration of all vital events including births,and deaths and issues relevant certificates as proof of such registration. Civil registration promotes efficient government planning, effective use of resources and aid and more accurate monitoring of progress towards achieving the sustainable development plan.


Death certificates are used to compile mortality statistics that are used to assess health, set public health goals and policies, and direct research and resources. The death certificate contains vital information about the deceased, as well as the circumstances surrounding his or her death and the cause of death.
